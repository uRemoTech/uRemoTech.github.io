Why uRemoTech?


1. remote work as the core of our work philosophy
Remote work is the cornerstone of our corporate structure, which we have deliberately chosen! We are convinced that flexible working hours and remote work promote and support the ideal balance between work and family.

2. with us you work on varied projects
You will work on challenging and diverse projects so that you can constantly develop and apply your expertise to different and exciting clients. This ensures that you continue to develop.

3. We live an open corporate culture
We value feedback, foster a culture of open communication and live in an atmosphere where constructive feedback is valued and encouraged. This approach, which focuses on transparency, inclusiveness and adaptability, means that we encourage the emergence of new ideas and are constantly evolving as a result.

4. we strive for technological excellence \
Our heart beats for innovation and technological excellence. Together with you, we develop high-quality and innovative solutions. We work with the latest tools to enable this technical excellence for our customers.

5. we offer you individual career development
In order to offer the best performance, it is important to develop and train yourself further. We encourage and challenge you so that you can grow into new challenges and take on more responsibility. We offer you individual development opportunities, training and further education to help you take the next step in your career.

6. sustainability through remote work
As a pioneering company that works remotely for the most part, we are proud to support sustainability in addition to technological excellence, because our working model significantly reduces the daily commute and makes a major contribution to our ecological footprint.
