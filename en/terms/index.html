for direct placement and project deployment of project personnel (B2B) – version date: 2025-08-23

1. Scope and Incorporation

1.1 These GTC apply vis-à-vis entrepreneurs (Section 14 BGB) to (a) the direct placement of candidates for permanent employment and (b) the project deployment of project personnel at the customer.

1.2 The GTC become part of the contract if uRemoTech refers to them in the offer (link/PDF) and the customer accepts the offer. Deviating terms of the customer do not apply without uRemoTech’s express consent in text form.

1.3 The version of the GTC linked in the offer, with its version date, applies. uRemoTech archives previous versions.

2. Definitions

“Introduction” means any transmission or naming of a candidate/project-personnel profile by uRemoTech—regardless of the communication channel.

“Project personnel” are uRemoTech’s own employees or contractually engaged specialists.

“Affiliated companies” are those within the meaning of Section 15 of the German Stock Corporation Act (AktG).

3. uRemoTech’s Services

3.1 Identification, interviews, and introduction of suitable candidates/project personnel according to the customer’s requirements.

3.2 Suitability check based on candidate-provided information; an in-depth verification (e.g., authenticity of qualifications) is not owed.

3.3 No particular placement or project success is owed. uRemoTech also makes no warranty regarding a candidate’s qualifications.

4. Customer Duties / Non-Disclosure to Third Parties

4.1 The customer shall inform uRemoTech without undue delay, but no later than within 7 days of receipt, if an introduced candidate/project personnel is already known. Any objection on the grounds of prior knowledge is excluded after this period.

4.2 Profiles/information may not be passed on to third parties without uRemoTech’s prior consent; the same applies to affiliated companies.

5. Direct Placement (Permanent Employment)

5.1 Fee Accrual. The fee base (amount exclusively as per the offer) arises upon conclusion of an employment contract between the customer (or a third party after onward transfer/affiliated company) and the introduced candidate.

5.2 Tail Period. If within 12 months after the most recent introduction a contract with the introduced candidate is concluded—whether as permanent employment, service/work contract, employee leasing, or any other engagement—this is deemed facilitated by uRemoTech and is subject to the fee as per the offer; this also applies where engagement occurs via affiliated companies or third parties.

5.3 Anti-Circumvention and Liquidated Damages. Prohibited in particular are (i) onward transfer of profiles followed by engagement/hiring, (ii) contracting via intermediaries, affiliated companies, or related third parties, (iii) changing the contract model within the tail period. For violations, the customer owes liquidated damages equal to the fee that would have accrued under the most recent uRemoTech offer in the case of proper engagement; proof of higher or lower damages remains reserved.

5.4 Invoicing & Due Date. Invoicing after the customer’s notification of contract and key terms; payment term 14 days net; statutory default rules (Sections 286, 288 BGB) apply.

6. Project Deployment of Project Personnel (Service Agreement with uRemoTech)

6.1 Contract Model. The customer concludes the service/project agreement exclusively with uRemoTech; the project personnel has no contractual relationship with the customer. Project details (performance/time records, invoicing, payment terms, cooperation) are governed solely by the service agreement/offer.

6.2 Non-Solicitation/Anti-Circumvention. The customer undertakes not to enter into direct contracts with the deployed project personnel during the ongoing project and for 12 months thereafter, and not to arrange circumvention via third parties. In case of breach, the customer owes liquidated damages equal to the lost project revenue, but at least the daily rate for 30 project days; proof of higher or lower damages remains reserved.

7. Information and Verification Duties

The customer shall inform uRemoTech without undue delay in text form of the conclusion and key terms of contracts/engagements with introduced persons. To verify compliance with these GTC, uRemoTech may, where there is a specific reason, request inspection of objectively necessary, appropriate, and proportionate evidence (e.g., engagement/employment confirmations, anonymized deployment records). The customer’s trade and business secrets remain protected.

In cases of slight negligence, liability arises only for breach of essential contractual duties and is limited to typical, foreseeable damages; indirect damages/lost profits are excluded. Unaffected: product liability and injury to life, body, or health; applies also in favor of legal representatives/vicarious agents.
The limitation period for defect and damage claims is 12 months from the date the defect or damage becomes known.

9.2 Applicable data protection laws (in particular the GDPR) apply. Responsibilities and any required agreements (e.g., data processing agreements) are defined on a project-specific basis.
