Systemadministrator / IT- Consultant (m/w/d)

Vermittlung | 70% Remote | Festanstellung | Vollzeit

We are currently looking for an experienced System Administrator / IT Consultant (m/f/d) to support the team of one of our important customers. The position is a permanent position.

Tasks

  • Ensuring the operation of business and process IT
  • Planning, installing, and administering high-availability virtualization and server systems, storage environments, and IT infrastructures
  • Installing and configuring new servers, systems, and software
  • Participating in strategy, innovation, digitization, and change projects
  • Technical design of interfaces between technical applications in business and process IT and other departments

Profile

  • Successfully completed training in the IT field or degree in information technology, computer science, or a comparable field
  • Several years of professional experience in system administration and working with complex IT infrastructures
  • Ideally, knowledge and experience in the area of IT security and firewall administration
  • Experience in IT project and process management
  • Knowledge in the field of virtualization environments, storage networks, and the Microsoft Windows Active Directory environment as well as Microsoft 365
  • Knowledge in the terminal server environment and knowledge in the Citrix environment desirable

Requirements

  • Strong teamwork and communication skills
  • Professional and friendly manner
  • High degree of initiative and motivation
  • Independent, solution-oriented, and structured approach to work
  • Customer- and service-oriented thinking and acting
  • Class B driver’s license
  • Fluency in German is required

General conditions

  • Start date: Immediately
  • Location: Westerstede – office, field work, and up to 70% remote.
  • Salary range: 50,000 - 72,000 euros

Benefits

  • Work in an interesting customer environment
  • Continuous training and development
  • A collegial and respectful working atmosphere with an open culture of communication and flat hierarchies
  • Work in a young, qualified, and committed team
  • Performance-based remuneration
